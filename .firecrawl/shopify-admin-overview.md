# Your account

When you create a Shopify store, you create a Shopify account. Your Shopify account connects all stores and organizations associated for your account, for stores that you own, or stores that you have a user account on.

From your Shopify account, you can manage your account details, security settings, and access stores associated with your account.

The following pages in the [**Settings**](https://admin.shopify.com/settings) menu of your Shopify admin are connected to your Shopify account:

- [**Billing**](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/billing): View and manage bills for your [store](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/manage-billing) or [organization](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/organization-settings/billing) from this page.
- [**Plan**](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/plan): Manage [store plans](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/manage-orgs-and-stores/manage-pricing-plan), including changing your plan, pausing or deactivating your plan, or re-activating a frozen store from this page.
- [**Users**](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/organization-account): Manage [users](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/users) in your store or organization from this page.
- [**Organization**](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/organization-details): If you're on the Shopify Plus plan, or have an organization, manage [your organization name](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/manage-orgs-and-stores/manage-org-settings) and view stores in your organization.
- [**General**](https://admin.shopify.com/settings/general): Manage [store details](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/manage-orgs-and-stores/manage-store-details), [group a store](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/manage-orgs-and-stores/manage-orgs/group-store) to an organization, or [change or transfer ownership](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/your-account/manage-orgs-and-stores/change-transfer-ownership) outside of your existing users from this page.

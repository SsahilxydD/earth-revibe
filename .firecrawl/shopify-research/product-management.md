# Products

The **Products** section in your Shopify admin helps you manage your store's entire product catalog, from basic product setup to advanced customization. There are also automation, AI, and bulk management tools that can help you save time when working on your business's product offerings.

## On this page

- [Features of the Products section](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products#features-of-the-products-section)
- [Connections to other Shopify admin sections](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products#connections-to-other-shopify-admin-sections)

## Features of the Products section

At minimum, a product in Shopify needs to have only two things: A title and a price. From there, you can add descriptions, images, videos. compare-at prices, inventory levels, variants, tags, metafields, and many other details that help you display and sell your products.

### Product details

When you add a product to Shopify, you can configure all the relevant settings for your product:

- Title and description: Craft SEO-friendly product names and descriptions using rich text formatting. Avoid duplicate manufacturer descriptions for better search visibility.
- Media: Upload images, 3D models, or videos to demonstrate products. Supports desktop and mobile layout adjustments and zoom functionality.
- Pricing and inventory: Set base prices, track stock quantities, and manage variant-specific inventory.

For a complete list of details and step-by-step instructions, refer to the [product details documentation](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products/details/product-details-page).

### Product management

After you add your products, you can manage them individually or through bulk actions. You can organize them into collections to help apply certain settings to groups of products or display them together on your online store.

Review the following product management actions for more details and instructions:

- [Adding and updating products](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products/add-update-products)
- [Organizing products into collections](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products/collections)
- [Creating metafields for customized product details](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/custom-data/metafields)
- [Using product tags to group products](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/shopify-flow/reference/actions/add-product-tags)

You can also apply certain changes to many products at the same time, such as adjusting prices or changing sales channel visibility using the [bulk editor](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/shopify-admin/productivity-tools/bulk-editing). You can also make changes to your product catalog using [CSV files](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products/import-export/using-csv).

## Connections to other Shopify admin sections

When your product catalog is set up, there are other areas in your Shopify admin that can reference these products to help set up the rest of your store:

- [Inventory and variants](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/products/inventory/setup): Manage stock levels per variant combination (size and color).
- [Online store menus](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/online-store/menus-and-links/editing-menus): Use smart or manual collections to power store menus.
- [Shipping settings](https://help.shopify.com/en/manual/fulfillment/setup): Create product-based rules for your shipping rates, such as basing them off of product price or weight.
